Who We Are

HealthConnect is a student-run wellness organization dedicated to creating a healthier, more balanced campus community. We bring together students who are passionate about fitness, mental health awareness, nutrition, and overall well-being, and we provide accessible resources and events for the entire student body — members and non-members alike.

Our Mission

To empower students with the tools, knowledge, and community support they need to build sustainable, healthy habits — physically, mentally, and socially — throughout their college journey and beyond.

Our Vision

A campus culture where wellness is woven into everyday student life, where no one feels alone in their health journey, and where every student has access to the resources they need to thrive.

Our Core Values

Inclusivity

Welcoming all fitness levels and backgrounds.

Community

Fostering supportive, lasting connections.

Balance

Prioritizing well-being alongside academics.

Accessibility

Providing free or low-cost wellness resources.

Education

Sharing evidence-based health knowledge.
